About Us
Who We Are VaxHeroMoms is a dedicated non-profit organization dedicated to promoting Hepatitis B vaccination and raising awareness about its importance in Nigeria and beyond. Our team consists of 4 passionate medical students, united by a common goal: to eradicate Hepatitis B and its devastating effects.
Our Journey Founded in 2024, VaxHeroMoms has been at the forefront of the fight against Hepatitis B, working tirelessly to address the gaps in vaccination coverage, combat stigma and discrimination, and empower mothers and babies in communities to take control of their health. Over the years, we have grown and evolved, expanding our reach, strengthening our partnerships, and making a tangible impact in the lives of those affected by Hepatitis B.
Our Values At VaxHeroMoms, we are guided by the following core values: Compassion: We approach our work with empathy and understanding, recognizing the challenges faced by individuals affected by hepatitis B and striving to support them with compassion and dignity. Collaboration: We believe in the power of collaboration and partnership, working hand-in-hand with communities, governments, healthcare providers, and other stakeholders to achieve our shared goals. Integrity: We uphold the highest standards of integrity, transparency, and accountability in everything we do, earning the trust and confidence of those we serve and collaborate with.
Mission
At VaxHeroMoms, our mission is to: Educate: We strive to educate individuals and communities about the risks of hepatitis B and the benefits of vaccination through informative campaigns, workshops, and outreach programs. Advocate: We advocate for policies and initiatives that support universal access to hepatitis B vaccination, especially in underserved communities. Empower: We empower individuals to take control of their health by providing access to resources, support networks, and vaccination services. Why Hepatitis B Vaccination Matters Hepatitis B is a serious viral infection that can lead to liver damage, liver cancer, and even death if left untreated. Vaccination is the most effective way to prevent hepatitis B transmission and its potentially life-threatening consequences. By promoting vaccination, we aim to protect individuals and communities from the burden of hepatitis B-related diseases. Our Impact Since our establishment, we have made significant strides in our efforts to promote hepatitis B vaccination: Increased Awareness: We have reached thousands of individuals through social media awareness campaigns, educational materials, and community events, helping to dispel myths and misconceptions about Hepatitis B. Improved Access: Through partnerships with healthcare providers and government agencies, we have facilitated access to vaccination services in remote and underserved areas, ensuring that no one is left behind. Together, we can make a difference in the fight against hepatitis B and ensure a healthier future for all.
